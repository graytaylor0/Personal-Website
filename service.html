Other Work

  • Adaptive Driving Access
    • May 2018 - Aug 2018

ADA converts vans into wheelchair accessible vehicles. Not only do they make it easy for non-drivers in wheelchairs to access their vehicles, they also make it possible for those in a wheelchair to drive. They do this by removing the driver's seat and installing hand controls into a vehicle. A lock mechanism is installed on the floor of the driver's seat, and their wheelchair will lock right in place!
